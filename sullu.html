Story-
Sulochana Dubey, nicknamed Sulu, is a housewife who enjoys her life to the fullest with her husband and 11-year-old son.
She dreams of being a working woman, but since she couldn't 
complete her high school she cannot think of any blue collar jobs.
Her two elder twin sisters are always critical of her educational credentials and mock her failed business ideas.
Sulu is fond of participating and winning any contest around her, be it a singing competition or an athletic event.
She is a voice artist in her own right and is able to mimic a few people around her.
