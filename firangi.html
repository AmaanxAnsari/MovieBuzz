Story-
Manga was born with a unique ability that he could cure anyone's backache with a simple kick to their backside.
One day he manages to cure Mark Daniel (Edward Sonnenblick) of his backache using the same ability of his.
Impressed by this act of Manga, Mark offers him a job in the Police Force, which Manga happily accepts.
Manga is now convinced that since he has a job now, Sargi's family won't object to his marriage with her 
and so he approaches Sargi's grandfather Lalaji (Aanjjan Srivastav) with regards to this. 
But Lalaji, who is a follower of Mahatma Gandhi refuses the proposal because Manga is working for the Britishers
the very same people from whom the people of the country are fighting to get their independence from.
