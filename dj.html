Story-
Duvvada Jagannatham Sastry (Allu Arjun) is a small town Shaiva Brahmin Pujari boy who goes against all odds. 
Seeing this quality of his, a high-profile cop (Murali Sharma) asks him to become DJ, a man who fights crime.
This is the same time when Jagannadham falls in love with Pooja (Pooja Hegde) the daughter of minister.
(Posani Krishna Murali) involved with yet another crime lord Royyala Naidu (Rao Ramesh) operates in disguise. 
On the other hand, DJ decides to take on Royyala Naidu in his mission but does not know he is in actual life.
Rest of the story is as to how Duvvada Jagannadham alias DJ manages to finish Royyala Naidu.
